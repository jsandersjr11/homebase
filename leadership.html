Leadership & Strategy

Building High-Performance Teams & Scalable Systems

With 10+ years of experience, I’ve transitioned from a craft-focused designer to a servant leader who aligns Product, Engineering, and GTM teams. My focus is on delivering measurable business outcomes—increasing revenue by $2M+/yr and reducing release cycles by 20%—through culture, process, and intelligent design systems.

Team & Culture

Hiring for culture add, not just culture fit. Mentorship ladders allow designers to grow from individual contributors to strategic partners.
Attracting & Retaining Talent
I believe in building teams where designers feel empowered to take ownership. At Clearlink and repeatGROWTH, I led the hiring and mentorship of cross-functional design teams, establishing clear career ladders and regular critique rituals that reduced ego and increased collaboration.
Servant Leadership
My role is to unblock my team. I partner with executives to prioritize UX initiatives—like the ones impacting 3M+ monthly users at Clearlink—ensuring my team works on high-impact problems rather than feature factories.

Process & Agile

Integrating design into the heartbeat of development. Speed and quality are not mutually exclusive.
Dual-Track Agile
I’ve implemented DesignOps models that reduced product release cycles by 20%. By running discovery (research/testing) ahead of delivery (engineering sprints), we ensure that developers are building validated solutions, minimizing rework and "design debt".
AI-Enhanced Workflows
To further accelerate our speed-to-insights, I embedded AI tools like Claude Sonnet and Figma AI into our workflow. This improved our research synthesis and ideation speed by 30%, allowing the team to spend more time on high-level strategy and less on rote production tasks.

Systems & Strategy

Bridging the gap between design and code.
Implementing shadcn/ui
At Clearlink, I led the implementation of shadcn/ui to optimize our design-to-development process. By adopting a standardized, accessible-from-the-start component library, we drastically reduced the "translation loss" between Figma and React. This allowed engineers to copy-paste code that matched our design tokens exactly, accelerating build times and ensuring UI consistency at scale.
Design Systems as a Product
I view the Design System not just as a UI kit, but as an internal product. At Symplr, I implemented systems across 4 enterprise applications to ensure HIPAA compliance and consistency. This systematic approach allows us to scale compliant patterns instantly across the organization.
Business Impact
Strategy means driving numbers. By leading UX and CRO strategies rooted in these systems, I helped drive a $2M+/year revenue increase at Clearlink through optimized conversion funnels and data-driven design refinements.
