Kareo Treatment Plans

About the Project

Mental health providers offer services to support an individual's mental health and to help treat mental and behavioral disorders. One of the tools providers use to effectively treat patients and track progress is a treatment plan. The purpose of a treatment plan is to help guide a patient towards reaching goals and help providers to monitor progress and make treatment adjustments when necessary. The treatment plan is a detailed plan tailored to the individual patient and is a powerful tool for engaging the patient in their treatment.

Discovery Research
When developing a research plan, the first thing I always ask my team is “What is it that we want to learn?” I document all our questions as well as assumptions that need to be validated. Based on this list, I evaluate the most appropriate research methods to help us answer the questions we write down.

Based on the questions we had, we did the following:
- Conducted 7 customer interviews to understand their current treatment planning process
- Sent a survey to help us understand needs when it came to documenting treatment
- Met with Sales reps to understand what prospect customers’ asks

Define

The right measurable outcome is based on understanding the needs, perceptions, and experiences of those we are attempting to serve.
User Problem
Because of a lack of treatment plan note type / template providers don't have the proper tools to build a structured and coherent treatment plan or an easy way to review a it during the patient visit. During a typical visit therapists will document progress / adjust it if necessary to satisfy the demands of HMOs, managed care companies, third-party payers, and state and federal agencies. Because of the lack of Treatment Plans providers and patients also don't have the ability to electronically sign a it or get alerted when it's time review on update the treatment plan.
Business Problem
One of the top sales blockers in Mental Health includes the lack of treatment plan functionality in Kareo. Treatment plans are essential for Mental Health providers to providing well-rounded care by helping them build a clear, actionable plan for patients that can be monitored during treatment.

Prototyping through iteration
Iterating by creating multiple prototype versions was essential to making sure we got the treatment plans feature right due to the complexity of some patients treatment and the time commitment from our engineers.
